FAQ

Common questions about Pulse prom booking.

What ticket types are available?
Each prom offers three ticket types: Lounge (premium seating), VIP (exclusive access), and Standing (general admission). Prices vary by prom and ticket type.
Which proms can I book?
Pulse offers booking for four proms: EBIS Prom, AIS, CES, and NIS. Each prom has its own date, venue, and pricing.
Can I change my ticket type after booking?
Please contact Pulse support for any changes to your booking. Availability depends on remaining tickets.
